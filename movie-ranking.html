Amber's Top Movies

A list of the top 10 movies of all time according to Amber

Howl's Moving Castle

This is one of my favourite movies because of the amazing detail put into the artwork in each scene.

Grease

I love this film because growing up Me, my Mum and Grandma would watch it together.

10 Things I Hate About You

This film is the perfect Rom-Com with a heart-warming ending.

Star Wars: prequals

The star wars prequals are by far the best movies in the star wars franchise with the perfect balance of romance, sci-fi and politics.

Murder Mystery

Because who doesn't love a movie with Adam Sandler and Jenifer Aniston in.

Knive's Out

This is the perfect film for crime lovers with a light balance of comedy and lots of juicy drama.

Us

One of the best horror movies i have ever watched. You'll be on the edge of your seat for the entire movie and when you finally think you know what's going to happen, Jordan Peele will Prove you wrong.

Halloween

It is simply just a classic timeless horror that i love to watch every halloween.

White Chicks

White Chicks is a great chick flick and commedy movie to watch with your friends for a laugh.

Spirited Away

I love all Studio Ghibli movies really but this one holds a special place in my heart.
